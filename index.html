FEFO + MRSL Rules

📦 FEFO Rule

First Expiring, First Out — batches with the earliest expiry dates are selected first for dispatch, minimizing waste.

⏱ MRSL Rule

Minimum Remaining Shelf Life — a batch must have ≥ 20% of its total shelf life remaining AND ≥ 30 days to expiry (configurable) to be approved for dispatch.
